Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular dish of Italian origin, beloved by people and a particularly famous orange cat. This recipe here is taken from The Better Homes and Gardens New Cook Book, 16th Edition.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • 12 oz ground beef
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 14.5 oz can of diced tomatoes
  • 8 oz tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon dried Italian seasoning
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup jarred roasted red sweet peppers
  • 2 cups cheese

Steps

  1. Cook noodles according to paclkage directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large saucepan cook desired meat, onion, and garlic until meat is browned, stirring to break up meat as it cooks. Drain.
  3. Stir Tomatoes and seasomning into meat mizture. Bring to boiling. Reduce heat, Simmer uncovered for 15 minutes, stirring ocassionally.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. For filling, combine egg, diary, and the 1/4 Parmesan cheese. Set aside.
  5. Spread 1/2 cup of meat sauce over the bottom of a 2 quart rectangular baking dish. Layer half of the Noodles in the dish, overlapping and trimming to fit. Spread with half of cheese filling. Top with half of the remaining sauce, half of the roasted sweet peppers, and half of the cheese. Repeat lauers. If desired, sprinkle with additional Parmesan.
  6. Place baking dish on a baking shet. Bake, uncovered for 40 to 45 minutes or until heated through and bubbly. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
